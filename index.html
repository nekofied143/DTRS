How to Use the DTR Tool

  1. Fill in your Name, Month / Year, and In Charge name.
  2. Click Add Schedule to choose dates and a schedule template.
  3. Enter your AM/PM times, and use Next Day Out for overnight completion.
  4. Click Generate DTR to preview the record.
  5. Use Export to Excel to save a CSV copy, or Print for a formatted report.
  6. Use Import from Excel to reload saved records.

Only scheduled working days allow time entry. Non-working days are automatically disabled.
